NSI Bitcoin Simulator was my first "middle scale" Python project.

The purpose was to create a little incremental game, with 2 of my friends, when we were in middle school.
During two or three months, we managed to setup a little graphic interface and a few functionnalities for our game.
The game is automatically downloading the Python modules necessary for its proper functioning, and it is fully scalable, allowing it to accept a vast majority of screen resolutions.
I led the group, providing instructions to my teammates during the execution of the project. I made the game's textures on Photoshop, and I created the main part of the code.
